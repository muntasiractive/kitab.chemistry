# Introduction

Welcome to **Kitab.Chemistry**, an open-source documentation database dedicated to Chemistry.

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive, community-driven resource for learners and researchers of all levels, covering everything from organic chemistry to physical chemistry and biochemistry.

## Our Goal

To create a centralized, searchable, and collaborative knowledge base that empowers students and enthusiasts to understand the building blocks of our world.
